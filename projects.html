Marina Plaza

3D Modeling · Environmental Design

A harbor-side plaza designed to feel calm, open, and story-like, with layered terraces, soft lighting, and a strong focus on atmosphere. The project explores how architecture can frame movement and daily rituals in a coastal setting.

3D Train Journey

Rhino · 3D Modeling

A stylized train model inspired by animated films. The project focuses on proportions, detailing, and materials to capture a sense of gentle travel and nostalgia, like a scene paused between stations.

Interior Space Study

A series of interior vignettes exploring how light, color, and simple forms can create a quiet, protective mood. The work experiments with camera angles and composition to direct the viewer’s attention through the space.
