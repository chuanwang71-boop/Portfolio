About

Hi, I’m Chuan Wang.

I’m a visual design student at Louisiana State University, with an earlier background in landscape design. That combination shaped the way I see composition, atmosphere, and the emotional weight of space—whether physical or digital.

My interest sits between web & interaction design, 3D environmental scenes, and mapping-based storytelling. I enjoy building quiet, cinematic moments—interfaces and spaces that feel like small worlds, inspired by gentle light, nature, and Ghibli-like atmospheres.

What I’m interested in

  • Designing calm, cinematic web experiences
  • Bringing landscape thinking into digital visual design
  • Mapping & GIS as tools for storytelling
  • Exploring 3D objects, interiors, and environmental atmospheres

Skills

  • HTML · CSS · JavaScript
  • Web Animation
  • ArcGIS & Mapping
  • Rhino 3D Modeling
  • Adobe Creative Suite
